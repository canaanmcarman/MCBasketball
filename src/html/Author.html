Canaan Stockwell is an OK dude who has been coding since he was 3 years old. He knows over 30 languages and loves blogging about them! He has made it his life mission to educate the masses about coding and prove that anyone can do it. His main hobbies are reading (programming books) , traveling (to new coding conferences), and meeting new people (usually fellow devs). His favorite band is Radiohead and his favorite album by them is OK Computer (of course!). When he isn't coding he just wants to spend time with his family and friends who beg him to stop talking about code. But he loves it more than anything!
